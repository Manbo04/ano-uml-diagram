🔐 Authentication & Signup

authlogin.pysignup.py

Two login flows: username/password (bcrypt) and Discord OAuth2. Signup initializes Economy 2.0 tables (user_economy, user_military), grants starter resources (15k steel, 10k components, 10k aluminium), creates first province, and sets initial policies. Rate limiting via signup_attempts table. reCAPTCHA on signup. Sessions permanent (365-day).

🏙 Province & Building Management

provinceprovince.pyaction_loop.py

Provinces have population demographics (children/working/elderly), happiness, productivity, pollution, energy, land. 36 building types across 6 categories: electricity (5), retail (7), public works (7), military (5), industry (9), processing (5). Building uses advisory locks for deadlock safety. City slots hold commercial buildings; land slots hold extractors. Energy system: producers vs consumers must balance. Rations distribution requires physical distribution buildings.

💰 Economy & Market

economymarket.pytrade_agreements.pycountries.py

15 resources + gold currency. Normalized schema: resource_dictionary + user_economy. Market: buy/sell offers, direct trades. Trade agreements: recurring automatic exchanges (1h-1wk intervals) with proposer/receiver/status/max_executions lifecycle. Tax income based on population x land x policy modifiers. Revenue: building production x productivity multiplier minus operating costs. Demographic consumption: children/working/elderly eat at different rates.

⚔ War & Combat System

warwars/attack_scripts/

12 unit types: soldiers, tanks, artillery, bombers, fighters, apaches, destroyers, cruisers, submarines, spies, ICBMs, nukes. War lifecycle: declare > choose 3 units > set amounts > fight > morale decremented > peace or war end. Supply regeneration: 50/hr, max 2000, with upgrade bonuses. Combat: 3-roll dice with unit bonuses. Casualty persistence via war_orchestrator. Infrastructure damage: random building destruction (1500 HP each). War end: 20% resource transfer from loser. Peace offers with demanded resources.

🤝 Coalitions & Diplomacy

socialcoalitions.pyintelligence.py

Coalition system: 8 roles (leader, co-leader, minister of x4, member, applicant). 27 routes. Coalition banking, treaties (NAP, MDP, trade), applications, flags/descriptions. Intelligence: spy missions with 12-hour cooldown, probability-based success.

🔬 Tech Tree, Upgrades & Policies

techupgrades.pypolicies.pyaction_loop.py

18 tech upgrades with prerequisite chains. Research costs components, uses advisory locks. Soldiers policy: 7 levels. Education policy: 6 levels. Phase 3 policies: universal healthcare, mandatory schooling, industrial subsidies, rationing program. Feature flags control phased rollout.
